Ryu Hyun-jin (born March 25, 1987) is a South Korean professional baseball pitcher for the Toronto Blue Jays of Major League Baseball (MLB). He previously played in MLB for the Los Angeles Dodgers.

Year Achievement
2013 MLB Debut with the Dodgers
2018 NLCS Champion
2019 MLB ERA Leader
2020 Signs with Toronto Blue Jays
